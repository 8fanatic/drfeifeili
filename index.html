Learn about Dr. Fei Fei Li

Dr. Fei Fei is an expert in the field of AI and receiver of numerous awards. She is currently interested in AI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Robotics, AI+Healthcare, and Human Vision. From breaking barriers to numerous Scientific advancements, this is a timeline of Dr. Fei Fei Li's life and work created by Andrew Danforth and Braeden Lagasse.



Keeping scrolling to advance!




















Dr. Fei Fei Li is born



Dr. Fei Fei Li was born in 1976 in Chengdu, China. She would spend most of her childhood there.





















Dr. Fei Fei Li moves to America



At the age of 15, Dr. Fei Fei Li moves to America with her parents. The new country would come with many new challenges for Dr. Fei Fei Li to face.





















Dr. Fei Fei Li's first years in America



The first couple of years in America for Dr. Fei Fei Li were not easy. Li had no choice but to work multiple jobs to support her education and her family. This included buying a dry cleaning buisness for her parents.





















Graduation



In 1999, Dr. Fei Fei Li graduates from Princeton. She receives her first BA degree in physics with high honors.





















PHD

In 2005, Dr. Fei Fei Li would receive a PHD in electrical engineering from the California Insitute of Technology (commonly referred to as "Caltech").





















ImageNet


Dr. Fei Fei Li would start ImageNet in 2006, which would evolve into a database of over 15 million images that assist with the development of Artifical Intelligence (AI).





















Dr. Fei Fei Li's first time at Stanford


Dr. Fei Fei Li would first join the prestigious Californian School as an assistant professor in 2009. This would mark the start of her career at Stanford.





















Dedicated to leading.

In 2015, Dr. Fei Fei Li would launch SAILORS, a summer camp at Stanford for 9th Grade girls. The camp introduces its members to AI, to hopefully encourage them to consider a path in the field.





















I'm Feeling Curious




In 2017, Dr. Fei Fei Li becomes the vice president of Google. Most notably she would work on Google Cloud.



A Nonprofit Movement


What was SAILORS in 2015 becomes the non-profit movement Ai4All in 2017. Ai4All stays true to its original misson: improve diversity in the field of Artifical Intelligence.





















Global Recognition

In 2019, Dr. Fei Fei Li would be recognized by National Geographic via the National Geographic Further Award. The Award is given to a leader pushing boundaries in their field - in Li's case, that would be the field of Artifical Intelligence. The award is well deserved, based on Li's many contributions to AI and the movements she has helped out on such as Ai4All.



Human Centered AI

Fei Fei Li would also become the co-founder of the Stanford Insitute of Human-Centered Artifical Intelligence in 2019. Commonly abbreviated as HAI, the goal of the Insitute is to research Artificial Intelligence and its impacts on the global community, as well as educating people on Artifical Intelligence.





















Welcome, Dr. Li

Fei Fei Li is inducted into the National Academy of Engineering in 2020. She was inducted for her major contributions to machine learning and Artifical Intelligence.



What's happening?

Dr. Li was added to Twitter's board of directors for "unparalleled expertise in engineering, computer science and artificial intelligence" according to Omid Kordestani, the Executive Chairman of Twitter. This is an impressive addition to the company's history and with this new position Fei Fei Li will be able to shatter even more boundaries.



Academy of Medecine

In 2020, Fei Fei Li is inducted into the National Academy of Medecine. Founded in 1970 and formerly called the Insitute of Medecine, the National Academy of Medecine is a nonprofit organization that provides objective advice on health.





















The Story doesn't end there.

Dr. Fei Fei Li has numerous awards & accolades that she has accumlated over her career. Although it would be impossible to provide all of them, we have compiled a list of her most noteworthy achievements here.

  • In total, Dr. Fei Fei Li has accumlated 28 honors and medals, many of which are for her contributions to AI.
